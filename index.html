    ONLINE MITHURU ISKOLE

    we develop smart mobile application to identified as a valuable solution for improving knowledge in Grade 1 and 2 primary students' subjects of Mathematics, Sinhala, and Environment studies.

    Project Scope

    Literature Survey

    Numerous e-learning apps for kids address a wide range of educational topics, but they frequently lack integration. For example, standalone apps focus solely on object detection rather than combining the other learning domains mentioned earlier. To close the gap, we conducted separate background research and literature reviews for each of the four major components proposed for this application.

    The system is based on solid theories and helps kids learn important skills like drawing, writing, and recognizing numbers. It encourages them to learn on their own and keeps track of their progress carefully, letting parents know how they're doing. It provides a structured but flexible setup, helping kids grow both academically and emotionally.

    This mobile app is designed for primary school students to help them learn in a way that adapts to their needs in English. It uses technology, which kids are comfortable with, to develop their talents. For math, there are fun games to solve basic math problems at the child's skill level. In environmental skills, the app asks kids to identify things around them, starting from the house to the garden, using object detection. For language skills, there are activities to improve pronunciation using audio processing. And the app verifies a child's online achievements using Non-Fungible Token technology.

    Research Gap

    Following areas are the research gaps found in most of the recent researches.

    Mathematics Skills

    There is no app that provides mathematical tutorials with image processing , a handwriting recognition system and basic mathematical functions to Sinhala-speaking children in grade 1 and 2.

    Sinhala Skills

    There is currently no app in Sri Lanka based on grades one and two, made in Sinhala to write Sinhala letters.

    Environment Skills

    There aren't any app to deliver lessons to environment study with image recognition and AR technology for grade 1 & 2 students in Sinhala language

    Research Problem & Solution

    Proposed Problem

    How can technology be effectively utilized to enhance learning outcomes and student engagement in primary education in Sri Lanka, particularly for students in grades 1 and 2?

    Despite the availability of online learning platforms and educational resources, there is a significant gap in solutions tailored for young primary school students in Sri Lanka. This gap has been exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic, highlighting the urgent need for innovative approaches to support remote learning and address the diverse needs of young learners. The primary challenge is to understand the specific learning difficulties faced by these students, identify effective educational interventions, and leverage technological advancements, such as Augmented Reality (AR), to create engaging and interactive learning experiences.

    Proposed Solution

    'Mithuru Iskole' is a mobile application designed to enhance the quality of primary education in Sri Lanka by addressing the identified challenges. The app focuses on improving student engagement and learning outcomes through the integration of advanced technologies like AR. Key features of the app include:

    • Interactive Learning Experiences: Using AR, the app creates immersive learning environments that make educational content more engaging and understandable for young students.
    • Tailored Educational Interventions: The app is designed to address the specific learning needs of students in grades 1 and 2, providing customized content that aligns with their curriculum and learning pace.
    • Equitable Access: 'Mithuru Iskole' ensures that all students, regardless of their socio-economic background, have access to high-quality educational resources through mobile technology.
    • Impact Assessment: Measures will be developed to evaluate the app's effectiveness in improving student engagement, academic achievement, and overall learning outcomes. This includes tracking usage patterns, learning progress, and feedback from students and teachers.

      • By implementing 'Mithuru Iskole', the research aims to provide a scalable solution that promotes active learning, fosters student engagement, and ultimately improves the quality of primary education in Sri Lanka. The app's impact on educational outcomes will be continuously assessed to refine and enhance its features, ensuring it meets the evolving needs of young learners.

    Research Objectives

    Main Objective

    To create an interactive, efficient, and effective educational solution for students in Grade 1 and 2, focusing on Sinhala, Mathematics, and Environmental Studies subjects, enhancing their learning experiences through the Sinhala medium.

    Mathematics Skills

    To use image processing and machine learning to engage primary grade 1 and 2 school students in mathematics education in a fun and interactive way.

    Sinhala Skills

    Develop an interactive platform in Sinhala for children in grades one and two to improve their basic reading and writing skills. Investigate the efficacy, perceptions, challenges, and learning outcomes of online learning platforms for primary school students in Sri Lanka.

    Environment Skills

    To use image processing and machine learning and AR technology to engage primary grade 1 and 2 school students in environmental education in a fun and interactive way.

    Methodology

    Figure 2. High Level Architecture of the system.

    The proposed system consists of 3 main components. They are:

    • Sinhala Skills Module
    • Math Skills Module
    • Environment Skills Module

    In the system shown in Fig 2, students first learn the Sinhala alphabet and practice writing the characters. The system evaluates their handwriting and gives personalized feedback. If a student struggles, they are encouraged to try again and engage in activities to memorize the letters. Similarly, in the Sinhala Pronunciation module, students pronounce words or phrases, and the system records and evaluates their pronunciation, categorizing it as low, intermediate, or high proficiency.

    Once a child successfully captures a photo within the given time, the app's algorithm analyzes the image to see if it matches the target object. The app then displays a visually appealing result with detailed information about the object. Next, the child can participate in fun exercises to identify a variety of animals and household items that might not be found at home.

    This app creates fun and interactive math problems for kids, covering addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Instead of boring worksheets, kids solve problems on their phone or tablet by choosing the correct answer from the given options. It's an engaging way to improve math skills. The app also includes a tutorial section to teach kids how to identify and write numbers correctly, as well as perform basic math operations.
